Rock City

Massive rock formations along marked, scenic trails with a peaceful vibe.

Olean, NY

Worth exploring great views and trails great for photo opportunities. Overall great place to get away for part of the day to do some exploring and forget about the world. A very easy and not super strenuous hike which is great for families. I just wish there was more trail. It is a geological wonder and fun hike. Tons of old machinery, including out of commission oil well and one still working. With all the large rocks, there are plenty of placed to climb and find a seat.

They also had a great gift shop with reasonable prices. Customer service was amazing the owners are friendly and very welcoming and kind. Beautiful place to hike. Very peaceful and like being in an enchanted wonderland. Lots of crevices in the rocks to explore. Trail is about a mile and very well marked and maintained. The rock formations are really cool.
