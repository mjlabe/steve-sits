Private Paradise

Rocky but not too crowded beach with a breakwater to explore.

Westfield, NY

This hidden gem is sure to bring breathtaking views, wildlife, and good vibes. Kick if your shoes and let the sand work your tired, worked feet.

The beach is pretty secluded and didn't have a lot of people. It was enjoyable to walk down the beach and splash in the waves. Unfortunately there weren't many great spots to sit. Some benches near the lot and the rocks on the breakwater are about all you get.
